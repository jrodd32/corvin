Laminate

Laminate is a manufactured product that simulates the look of hardwood, ceramic tile, natural stone and other flooring. Well-suited for shoppers who want the aesthetic of real hardwood or stone without the cost or maintenance. Can be installed in or on all grade levels (even below grade) over many different subfloors and is easy to replace if desired.
