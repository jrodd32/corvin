Engineered Hardwood

Appearing similar to solid wood on surface, engineered wood floors are manufactured differently consisting of several thin wood plies laminated with glue, heat and pressure making them as durable as solid planks. Facing opposite directions, the plies counteract the natural growth & contracting that happen with moisture and humidity changes.
